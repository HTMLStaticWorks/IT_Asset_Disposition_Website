Our Secure Process

A transparent, audited chain of custody ensuring complete data security from start to finish.

01

Assessment & Logistics

We coordinate asset collection using GPS-tracked, secure logistics vehicles. Assets are scanned on-site for initial custody documentation.

02

Secure Intake & Auditing

Upon arrival at our ISO-certified processing facility, items undergo a rigorous secondary audit to match the manifest and enter our tracking portal.

03

Data Destruction

All media is wiped to NIST 800-88 specifications. Drives failing wipe procedures are physically shredded.

04

Yield & Reporting

Viable assets are remarketed globally. You receive a final report containing Certificates of Destruction and financial return statements.
